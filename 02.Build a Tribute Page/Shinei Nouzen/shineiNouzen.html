ABOUT

  • Name: Shinei Nouzen

  • Clan: Nouzen clan

  • Blood: Mixed of Pyrope and Onyx

  • He is the commander of spearhead squadron

  • Code name is Undertaker

  • He is also known as Reaper

BACKGROUND

  • Born on May 19th RY 351/SY 2132

  • His family used to lived in the First Sector of the Republic

  • Before the war with Giadian Empire,He used to live in sheltered life, surrounded by “only kindness and affection”

  • He also appeared to have developed his extrasensory ability at young age, which was hearing “voices that spoke without words” from his family

  • Then, the Empire of Giad, declared an all-out war against all of its neighboring countries, including the Republic of San Magnolia, the United Kingdom of Roa Gracia, and the Alliance of Wald started in SY 2139/RY 358

  • When the war begun, Shin and his family were forced to relocated to internment camp due to the Republic discriminatory policy

  • RY 359: The Legion attacks the Republic, leading to the death of Shin’s parents. Shin and his brother are sent to the internment camps. At that time Shin just turned eight , had not grasp the concept of death and asked his brother, Shourei to explained about his parents’ death

  • Due to anger and confusion, Shourei snapped and strangled him closed to death and blamed shin on his parents' death. Shourei action left scar on Shin neck and triggered Shin’s ability to hear voices of the dead

  • RY 360: Four years after enlistment, Shin begins hearing the voices of his brother’s which indicated that he was killed in action

  • RY 362: Shin joins the military and quickly rises through the ranks due to his exceptional skills

  • April, RY 367: Shin becomes the captain of the newly formed Spearhead Squadron

  • May 22, RY 367: Shin and the Spearhead Squadron receive their first Para-RAID synchronization from their new Handler, Vladilena Milizé an Alba noble and military officer known as Handler One

  • Shin tried to find Shourei voices that had been assimilated into Legion as Shepherd so he could bring closure to his brother and himself.

  • Once shin found his brother ,he fought with him .The fight almost took his life. However, help received just on time and Shin succeeded kill the Shepard

  • Shin cried emotionally as he finally abled to put his memories of his brother in rest and received closure that he needed for both of them.

  • After that, the members of Spearhead Squadron embarking new uncertain journey of freedom.
